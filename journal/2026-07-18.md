## What I learned
- When a search stalls on a value, first hunt the invariant (mod 4:
  absent), THEN exhaust. The Gray-code + walk-from-rounds trick
  turned "2^28 × cycle-count" from hopeless to ~8 minutes: you never
  need the full cycle count, only the length of rounds' own cycle,
  and early exit makes the average step count tiny.
- The classification is the real prize regardless of the sweep's
  verdict: the α-image landing pattern (0 → nowhere, 2 → four shared
  rooms, 4 → the α-fixed suites) is rigid enough that fifteen
  complements is all the mirror leaves standing at L = 343.
